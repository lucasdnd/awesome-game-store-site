Awesome Game Store is a procedurally generated Tycoon game where you manage a video game store throughout the 90s and beyond. Create your library of games, control pricing, buy consoles and manage your staff.

The game is scheduled for release in 2015 and will be available on Windows, Mac and Linux.

  • Procedurally generated content
  • Customize your store
  • Sell and rent games
  • Hire and manage staff
